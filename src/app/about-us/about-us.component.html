mission
Humanity has built bridges since the dawn of civilizations. Today, bridges are all around us - they provide an easier, faster and safer connection between two points. Bridges have evolved from mere structures to symbols of cities, nations, and human progress. The iconic bridge structures embody the engineers' eternal aspiration for greater achievement - longer and taller, stronger and faster. We all admire bridges that gracefully cross over a deep ravine, canyon, great river or bay and consider them highly technical achievements.
Today's bridges symbolize not only the advancement of our world in design but also its advancement in technology. As a result, architects and engineers have been able to combine design and technology to create bridges that are bigger, better, and more spectacular than ever before. Some of these are simply stunning - a veritable wonder of classical engineering capabilities and striking achievements of modern engineering. We have a mission to compile the most incredible bridges in the world, young and old.
